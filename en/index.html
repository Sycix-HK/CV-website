Civilization Cube

Civilization Cube is a solo strategy game for Android about managing a resource-filled rubik's cube

The cube has an automatic ecosystem with people interacting with the cube's resources on their own

The game's goal is to get a town to build a spacecraft in as few turns as possible by building them or pillaging it from other towns

2021 apr - 2021 jun

Haunted Waters

Haunted Waters is a naval combat based roguelike where you play as a ghost ship

You have different abilities including phase shifting as a dodge mechanic and an expandable playing card system that you can burn to use it's ability

You can use your collected souls to upgrade or heal your ship in the shop

2020 dec - 2021 mar

The Cardsmith

The Cardsmith is a story based first person role playing and a "collecting, playing and crafting card game"

In a world where cards have magical abilites, you play as a cardsmith, whose job is to immortalize dead legends in form of cards

Choices in main and side quests matter as you make friends and enemies, but you can always choose to just fill orders for your clients to make money and upgrade your workshop
